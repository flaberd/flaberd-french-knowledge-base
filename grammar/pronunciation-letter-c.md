# Prononciation de la lettre C (pronunciation of the letter C)

1. The combination **"ch"** gives the sound **[ʃ]** (like Ukrainian [ш]): *chat* [ʃa].
2. At the end of a word, C is usually **[k]**: *parc* [park].
   - It is silent after nasal vowels: *banc* [bɑ̃].
   - It is also silent in a few specific words: *porc* [pɔːʁ], *estomac* [ɛstɔma], *tabac* [taba].
3. **"Ç"** always gives **[s]**.
4. C gives **[s]** before **i, e, y**: *cirque* [siʁk]. In all other cases, C gives **[k]**: *cage* [kaːʒ].

## Mnemonic

**(I)** I **(E)** eat **(Y)** yogurt — C softens to [s] before the letters I, E, Y.

## Practice words

chat, chocolat, chaud, garçon, cœur, leçon, carnage, ça, ciment
