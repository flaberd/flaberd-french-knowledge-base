# Prononciation de la lettre G (pronunciation of the letter G)

1. **"g"** gives the sound **[ʒ]** (ж) before **i, e, y**: *cage*, *giraffe* (spelled this way in the source material; the standard French spelling is *girafe*). In all other cases, G gives **[g]** (like English "go", Ukrainian ґ): *garçon*.
   - Same rule as with the letter [C](pronunciation-letter-c.md) — mnemonic: **(I)** I **(E)** eat **(Y)** Yogurt.
2. **"gu"** + vowel = **[g]**: *guerre* [gɛːʁ].
3. **"gn"** = **[ɲ]** (like English "canyon", Ukrainian нь): *ligne* [liɲ].
