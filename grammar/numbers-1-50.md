## Formation pattern

- 11–16 (onze–seize) and 17–19 (dix-sept, dix-huit, dix-neuf) are their own words / dix-compounds.
- From 21, tens combine with units via a hyphen: vingt-deux, vingt-trois...
- The **×1** compounds (21, 31, 41) use **et** instead of a hyphen: vingt **et** un, trente **et** un, quarante **et** un.
