# L'accent tonique (word stress)

In French, stress always falls on the **last syllable** of a word (unlike Ukrainian or Russian, where stress can fall anywhere).

Example: *un cinéma* → [si.ne.ˈma] (stress on the final syllable "ma").

## Practice words

hôtel, animal, café, musique
