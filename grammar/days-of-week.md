# Days of the week (les jours de la semaine)

lundi, mardi, mercredi, jeudi, vendredi, samedi, dimanche.

All days of the week in French are **masculine**: le lundi, le mardi, le mercredi, le jeudi, le vendredi, le samedi, le dimanche.

## Nuance: "le" + day = recurring

"Le lundi" can also mean "every Monday" (a habitual/recurring event), as opposed to "ce lundi" (this specific Monday): "Je travaille le lundi" (I work [every] Monday) vs. "Je travaille ce lundi" (I work this Monday).
