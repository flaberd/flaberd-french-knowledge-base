> **Un œuf**
> **Des œufs** *(fs on ne prononce pas!)*

*(new pronunciation point)* The singular "un œuf" is pronounced with a final [f] sound, but in the plural "des œufs" both the "f" of *œufs* and the final "s" are silent — a liaison/pronunciation exception the teacher calls out explicitly. New vocabulary entry: [œuf](../vocabulary/nouns/œuf.md), noting this pronunciation irregularity.
