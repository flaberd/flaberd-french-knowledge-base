## 8. Reprendre — conjugaison (left blank in source, "???")

The slide gives only the pronoun column, with "???" for every form — an exercise for the student to derive the conjugation by analogy with *prendre*. The regular derivation (re- + prendre stem) is: je reprends, tu reprends, il/elle/on reprend, nous reprenons, vous reprenez, ils/elles reprennent. This is confirmed by the filled-in homework in §11 below ("tu reprends"). New vocabulary entry: [vocabulary/verbs/group-3/reprendre.md](../vocabulary/verbs/group-3/reprendre.md).

# Notes

- This lesson introduces the present-tense conjugation of **prendre** and its compounds **apprendre** (with "à + infinitif"), **comprendre**, and **reprendre** — all sharing the same irregular pattern (je/tu ...ends, il/elle/on ...end, nous ...enons, vous ...enez, ils/elles ...ennent). Recorded in a new grammar file, [grammar/prendre-present.md](../grammar/prendre-present.md).
- §1–2 grade the "Ma routine quotidienne" homework assigned in [lesson_00025](lesson_00025.md). Numerous errors are flagged in red/orange in the source without an explicit corrected version provided; likely corrections are noted in brackets as agent-added supplementary explanation, clearly distinguished from the source text itself, per source-preservation rules.

- §3 introduces ensuite/puis as vocabulary alternatives to reduce repetition of "après".
- Nearly all fill-in-the-blank exercises were left blank in the source PDF; they are preserved as blanks here.
