## 2. Correction — devoir de lesson_00023, §11 (avoir + adjectifs avant le nom)

> Anna et Marc ont une bel histoire à raconter : leur famille a de anciennes traditions et ils ont des photos anciennes.

This is the student's completed answer, shown without correction marks in the source. **Open error, preserved as-is:** "une **bel** histoire" should be "une **belle** histoire" — *histoire* is feminine, so it takes the regular feminine form "belle", not the special masculine-before-vowel form "bel" (which is only for masculine nouns like *un bel homme*). See [grammar/adjective-agreement-feminine.md](../grammar/adjective-agreement-feminine.md). The rest of the sentence ("de anciennes traditions") correctly applies the des→de shift before a preposed adjective, per [grammar/adjective-position.md](../grammar/adjective-position.md).
