A short oral warm-up, functioning as a lead-in review before the written exam given in [lesson_00042](lesson_00042.md) (the slide's own closing line, "EO / Partitif Examen", makes this connection explicit). Each line is a teacher prompt for the student to answer aloud; no written answers are recorded in the source. The prompts reuse already-taught material rather than introducing anything new:

- "beaucoup de lait" — expression of quantity ([grammar/partitive-articles.md](../grammar/partitive-articles.md#52-after-an-expression-of-quantity)).
- "les pâtes ou le riz" — definite article with *préférer* ([grammar/definite-articles.md](../grammar/definite-articles.md#with-verbs-of-opinionpreference)).
- "vous n'avez pas faim" — negation with the *avoir faim* idiom.
- "Conjuguer finir" / "Chercher" — recap of [finir](../vocabulary/verbs/group-2/finir.md) (2e groupe) and [chercher](../vocabulary/verbs/group-1/chercher.md) (1er groupe) conjugation.
- "vous vous endormez... à minuit" — recap of [s'endormir](../vocabulary/verbs/group-3/sendormir.md), an irregular reflexive verb (built on *dormir*, not a 2e groupe verb despite the -ir ending).
