## 3. La lettre G (the letter G)

1. **"g"** gives the sound **[ʒ]** (ж) before **i, e, y**: *cage*, *giraffe* (as spelled in the source; standard French spelling is *girafe*). In all other cases, G gives **[g]** (like English "go", Ukrainian ґ): *garçon*.
   - Same rule as with the letter **C** — mnemonic: **(I)** I **(E)** eat **(Y)** Yogurt.
2. **"gu"** + vowel = **[g]**: *guerre* [gɛːʁ].
3. **"gn"** = **[ɲ]** (like English "canyon", Ukrainian нь): *ligne* [liɲ].
