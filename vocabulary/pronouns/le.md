# Basic Information

## French

le

## Part of Speech

Pronoun

## Ukrainian

його (займенник прямого додатка, чол.р.)

## English

him / it (direct object, masculine)

# Forms *(if applicable)*

## Reflexive Form

Elides to **l'** before a vowel or mute h.

---

# Examples

- Je le mange. *(le fruit → le)*
- Je vois le ciel. → Je le vois.
