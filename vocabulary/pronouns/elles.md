# Basic Information

## French

elles

## Part of Speech

Pronoun

## Ukrainian

вони (жін.)

## English

they (feminine only)

---

# Examples

- Elles sont italiennes.
- Léa et Ada sont grandes. Elles sont blondes.

---

# Notes

3rd person plural feminine subject pronoun. Used only if the entire group is feminine. See [grammar/personal-pronouns.md](../../grammar/personal-pronouns.md).
