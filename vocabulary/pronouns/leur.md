# Basic Information

## French

leur

## Part of Speech

Pronoun

## Ukrainian

їм

## English

to them (indirect object, plural)

---

# Examples

- Il leur dit bonjour. *(à eux / à elles → leur)*
- Ils leur disent la nouvelle. *(à eux → leur)*

---

# Notes

Indirect object (COI) pronoun, used for both "à eux" and "à elles" — not to be confused with the possessive adjective "leur/leurs" (their). See [grammar/indirect-object-pronouns.md](../../grammar/indirect-object-pronouns.md).
