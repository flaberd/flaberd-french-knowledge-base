# Basic Information

## French

te

## Part of Speech

Pronoun

## Ukrainian

тобі / тебе

## English

you (indirect/direct object, informal singular)

# Forms *(if applicable)*

## Reflexive Form

Elides to **t'** before a vowel or mute h (e.g. tu t'habilles — see [grammar/reflexive-verbs.md](../../grammar/reflexive-verbs.md)).

---

# Examples

- Je te dis la vérité. *(à toi → te)*
