# Basic Information

## French

on

## Part of Speech

Pronoun

## Ukrainian

ми / люди / хтось (розмовне)

## English

we / one / people (informal)

---

# Examples

- On est étudiants.
- On est jeune(s).

---

# Notes

Informal equivalent of "nous"; conjugates like "il/elle" (3rd person singular) even though it often carries plural meaning. See [grammar/personal-pronouns.md](../../grammar/personal-pronouns.md).
