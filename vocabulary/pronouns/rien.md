# Basic Information

## French

rien

## Part of Speech

Pronoun

## Ukrainian

нічого

## English

nothing

---

# Examples

- Il ne voit rien. Nous n'avons rien à dire.
- Rien ne bouge ici. *(rien as the grammatical subject)*

---

# Notes

Negative pronoun, used with "ne" (ne...rien). Can also appear as the subject of the sentence ("Rien ne..."), still paired with "ne" but without "pas". See [grammar/negation.md](../../grammar/negation.md).
