# Basic Information

## French

la

## Part of Speech

Pronoun

## Ukrainian

її (займенник прямого додатка, жін.р.)

## English

her / it (direct object, feminine)

# Forms *(if applicable)*

## Reflexive Form

Elides to **l'** before a vowel or mute h.

---

# Examples

- Tu la prends. *(la pomme → la)*
- Je la vois. *(Marie → la)*
- Tu prends la pomme. → Tu la prends.

---

# Notes

Direct object (COD) pronoun, feminine singular. Also used for people (*Marie → la*), not just things. See [grammar/direct-object-pronouns.md](../../grammar/direct-object-pronouns.md).
