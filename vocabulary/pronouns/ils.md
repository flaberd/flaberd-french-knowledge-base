# Basic Information

## French

ils

## Part of Speech

Pronoun

## Ukrainian

вони (чол.)

## English

they (masculine or mixed group)

---

# Examples

- Ils sont italiens.
- Max et Léo sont grands. Ils sont blonds.

---

# Notes

3rd person plural subject pronoun. Used if the group contains at least one masculine person or thing. See [grammar/personal-pronouns.md](../../grammar/personal-pronouns.md).
