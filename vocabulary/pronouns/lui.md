# Basic Information

## French

lui

## Part of Speech

Pronoun

## Ukrainian

йому / їй

## English

to him / to her (indirect object, singular)

---

# Examples

- Elle lui dit merci. *(à lui / à elle → lui)*
- Il lui dit un secret. *(à elle → lui)*

---

# Notes

Indirect object (COI) pronoun, used for both "à lui" and "à elle" (no gender distinction, unlike the COD pronouns le/la). See [grammar/indirect-object-pronouns.md](../../grammar/indirect-object-pronouns.md).
