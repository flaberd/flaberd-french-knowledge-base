# Basic Information

## French

personne

## Part of Speech

Pronoun

## Ukrainian

ніхто

## English

nobody, no one

---

# Examples

- Je ne connais personne ici. Elle ne voit personne.
- Personne ne parle ici. *(personne as the grammatical subject)*

---

# Notes

Negative pronoun, used with "ne" (ne...personne) — not to be confused with the noun "une personne" (a person), which is unrelated in this negative-pronoun sense. Can also appear as the subject of the sentence ("Personne ne..."), still paired with "ne" but without "pas". See [grammar/negation.md](../../grammar/negation.md).
