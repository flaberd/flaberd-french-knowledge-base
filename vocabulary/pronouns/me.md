# Basic Information

## French

me

## Part of Speech

Pronoun

## Ukrainian

мені / мене

## English

me (indirect/direct object)

# Forms *(if applicable)*

## Reflexive Form

Elides to **m'** before a vowel or mute h (e.g. je m'appelle — see [grammar/reflexive-verbs.md](../../grammar/reflexive-verbs.md)).

---

# Examples

- Il me dit bonjour. *(à moi → me)*
- Tu me dis bonjour. *(à moi → me)*

---

# Notes

Indirect object (COI) pronoun ("à moi"), taught alongside the reflexive/COI overlap. See [grammar/indirect-object-pronouns.md](../../grammar/indirect-object-pronouns.md).
