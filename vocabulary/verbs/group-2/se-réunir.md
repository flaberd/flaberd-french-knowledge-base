# Basic Information

## French

se réunir

## Part of Speech

Verb

## Ukrainian

збиратися (разом)

## English

to meet up / to gather

# Forms *(if applicable)*

## Verb Group

Group 2 (regular -ir, reflexive)

## Reflexive Form

se réunir

---

# Conjugation *(if applicable)*

Present tense: je me réunis, tu te réunis, il/elle/on se réunit, nous nous réunissons, vous vous réunissez, ils/elles se réunissent. See [grammar/second-group-verbs-present.md](../../../grammar/second-group-verbs-present.md) and [grammar/reflexive-verbs.md](../../../grammar/reflexive-verbs.md).
