# Basic Information

## French

vieillir

## Part of Speech

Verb

## Ukrainian

старіти

## English

to grow old / to age

# Forms *(if applicable)*

## Verb Group

Group 2 (regular -ir)

---

# Conjugation *(if applicable)*

Present tense: je vieillis, tu vieillis, il/elle/on vieillit, nous vieillissons, vous vieillissez, ils/elles vieillissent. See [grammar/second-group-verbs-present.md](../../../grammar/second-group-verbs-present.md).

---

# Examples

- Vieille → vieillir → Je vieillis → Vous vieillissez. *(astuce: verb formed from an adjective, expressing a transformation)*
- Je vieillis chaque jour un peu plus.
- Les enfants grandissent et les parents vieillissent.
