# Basic Information

## French

s'épanouir

## Part of Speech

Verb

## Ukrainian

розквітати / реалізовувати себе

## English

to flourish / to blossom (figuratively)

# Forms *(if applicable)*

## Verb Group

Group 2 (regular -ir, reflexive)
