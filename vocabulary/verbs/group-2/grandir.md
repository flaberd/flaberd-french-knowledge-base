# Basic Information

## French

grandir

## Part of Speech

Verb

## Ukrainian

рости / збільшуватися

## English

to grow (up)

# Forms *(if applicable)*

## Verb Group

Group 2 (regular -ir)

---

# Conjugation *(if applicable)*

Present tense (model verb for the 2e groupe): je grandis, tu grandis, il/elle/on grandit, nous grandissons, vous grandissez, ils/elles grandissent. See [grammar/second-group-verbs-present.md](../../../grammar/second-group-verbs-present.md).

---

# Examples

- Grande → grandir → Je grandis → Vous grandissez. *(astuce: verb formed from an adjective, expressing a transformation)*
- Les enfants grandissent et les parents vieillissent.

---

# Notes

Used as the primary model verb for teaching the 2e groupe present-tense conjugation pattern.
