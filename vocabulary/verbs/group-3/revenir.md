# Basic Information

## French

revenir

## Part of Speech

Verb

## Ukrainian

повертатися (назад)

## English

to come back

# Forms *(if applicable)*

## Verb Group

Group 3 (irregular, compound of [venir](venir.md))

## Past Participle

revenu(e) *(same pattern as venir → venu)*

---

# Examples

- (revenir) On ___ à pied. Ils ___. *(passé composé drill: "on est revenu(e)")*
- Il revient vers 9 h. *(Monsieur Dumas story, present tense)*

---

# Notes

Named explicitly as one of the "maison d'être" verbs' **compounds**, conjugated like its base verb [venir](venir.md) and taking **être** as the passé composé auxiliary. Present-tense conjugation not separately taught, but follows *venir*'s pattern (il revient, like il vient).
