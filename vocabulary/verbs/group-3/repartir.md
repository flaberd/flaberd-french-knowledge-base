# Basic Information

## French

repartir

## Part of Speech

Verb

## Ukrainian

вирушати знову

## English

to leave again, to set off again

# Forms *(if applicable)*

## Verb Group

Group 3 (irregular, compound of [partir](partir.md))

## Past Participle

reparti(e) *(same pattern as partir → parti)*

# Notes

Named explicitly as one of the "maison d'être" verbs' **compounds**, conjugated like its base verb [partir](partir.md) and taking **être** as the passé composé auxiliary. Present-tense conjugation not separately taught.
