# Basic Information

## French

s'en sortir

## Part of Speech

Verb

## Ukrainian

виплутатися, впоратися

## English

to get by, to cope, to make it out

# Forms *(if applicable)*

## Verb Group

Group 3 (irregular, pronominal compound of *sortir* with the pronoun "en")

---

# Conjugation *(if applicable)*

Conjugates like *sortir* (see [vocabulary/verbs/group-3/sortir.md](sortir.md)) with the added reflexive/adverbial pronouns "se" + "en": *je m'en sors, tu t'en sors, il s'en sort...*

---

# Examples

- "Quand tu crois enfin que tu t'en sors" *(from Stromae, "Alors on Danse")*
