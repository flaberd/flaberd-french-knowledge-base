# Basic Information

## French

reprendre

## Part of Speech

Verb

## Ukrainian

відновлювати, брати знову

## English

to resume, to take again/back

# Forms *(if applicable)*

## Verb Group

Group 3 (irregular, built on *prendre*)

# Conjugation *(if applicable)*

Present tense (derived by analogy with *prendre*; source slide gave only "???" placeholders, confirmed by the filled-in homework in [lesson_00026](../../../lessons/lesson_00026.md), which gives "tu reprends"):

| Pronoun | Form |
|---|---|
| Je | reprends |
| Tu | reprends |
| Il / Elle / On | reprend |
| Nous | reprenons |
| Vous | reprenez |
| Ils / Elles | reprennent |

Same pattern as *prendre*. See [grammar/prendre-present.md](../../../grammar/prendre-present.md).

---

# Examples

- Tu reprends le travail après la pause.
- Nous ne reprenons pas le travail demain. Ils reprennent leurs entraînements ce mois-ci.
- Pourquoi tu ne reprends pas tes études ?
