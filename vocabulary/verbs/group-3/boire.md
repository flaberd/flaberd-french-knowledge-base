# Basic Information

## French

boire

## Part of Speech

Verb

## Ukrainian

пити

## English

to drink

# Forms *(if applicable)*

## Verb Group

Group 3 (irregular, -re sub-type)

---

# Conjugation *(if applicable)*

Present tense:

| Pronoun | Form |
|---|---|
| Je | bois |
| Tu | bois |
| Il / Elle / On | boit |
| Nous | buvons |
| Vous | buvez |
| Ils / Elles | boivent |

The source flags a common mix-up in the nous/vous forms with [vouloir](vouloir.md): "Nous buvons, nous voulons" — don't confuse the two.
