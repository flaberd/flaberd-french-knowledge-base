# Basic Information

## French

devenir

## Part of Speech

Verb

## Ukrainian

ставати

## English

to become

# Forms *(if applicable)*

## Verb Group

Group 3 (irregular, compound of [venir](venir.md))

## Past Participle

devenu(e) *(same pattern as venir → venu)*

---

# Examples

- Obama ___ (devenir) le premier président noir des États-Unis. *(passé composé drill: "est devenu")*

---

# Notes

Named explicitly as one of the "maison d'être" verbs' **compounds** (rentrer, devenir, revenir, repartir), conjugated like its base verb [venir](venir.md) and taking **être** as the passé composé auxiliary. Present-tense conjugation not separately taught.
