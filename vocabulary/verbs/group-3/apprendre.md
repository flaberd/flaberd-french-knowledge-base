# Basic Information

## French

apprendre

## Part of Speech

Verb

## Ukrainian

вчити(ся)

## English

to learn

# Forms *(if applicable)*

## Verb Group

Group 3 (irregular, built on *prendre*)

---

# Conjugation *(if applicable)*

Present tense:

| Pronoun | Form |
|---|---|
| Je | apprends (j'apprends) |
| Tu | apprends |
| Il / Elle / On | apprend |
| Nous | apprenons |
| Vous | apprenez |
| Ils / Elles | apprennent |

Same pattern as *prendre*. See [grammar/prendre-present.md](../../../grammar/prendre-present.md).

---

# Required Prepositions *(if applicable)*

**Apprendre à** + infinitif — *apprendre à conduire*, *apprendre à cuisiner*.

---

# Examples

- J'apprends le français.
- Tu apprends vite, bravo !
- On apprend à conduire. Elles apprennent à cuisiner.
- Il apprend à jouer du violon.

---

# Notes

Taught alongside *prendre*, *comprendre*, and *reprendre* as one family of compounds sharing the same irregular conjugation pattern.
