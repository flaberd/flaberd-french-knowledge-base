# Basic Information

## French

partir

## Part of Speech

Verb

## Ukrainian

виїжджати, вирушати

## English

to leave, to depart

# Forms *(if applicable)*

## Verb Group

Group 3 (irregular, sortir-type)

## Past Participle

parti *(regular -ir → -i for this sub-type, like [sortir](sortir.md))*

---

# Examples

- Le train vient de partir. Nous devons attendre le prochain. *(passé récent example, [grammar/passe-recent.md](../../../grammar/passe-recent.md))*

---

# Notes

One of the "maison d'être" verbs taking **être** as the passé composé auxiliary. Present-tense conjugation not separately taught, but follows the same pattern as the already-conjugated [sortir](sortir.md) (je pars, tu pars, il part, nous partons, vous partez, ils partent).
