# Basic Information

## French

pleuvoir

## Part of Speech

Verb

## Ukrainian

йти (про дощ)

## English

to rain

# Forms *(if applicable)*

## Verb Group

Group 3 (irregular)

---

# Conjugation *(if applicable)*

Impersonal verb, used only in the third person singular with **il**: **il pleut** (it's raining/it rains). See [grammar/weather-expressions.md](../../../grammar/weather-expressions.md).

---

# Examples

- Il pleut. *(source slide, with the infinitive "Pleuvoir" given alongside)*
- Au printemps, il pleut souvent.
