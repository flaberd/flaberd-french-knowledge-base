# Basic Information

## French

avancer

## Part of Speech

Verb

## Ukrainian

просуватися / йти вперед

## English

to advance / to move forward

# Forms *(if applicable)*

## Verb Group

Group 1 (regular -er)

---

# Conjugation *(if applicable)*

Present tense: j'avance, tu avances, il/elle/on avance, nous avançons, vous avancez, ils/elles avancent.

---

# Irregularities *(if applicable)*

-cer verb: the nous form takes a cedilla (avançons). See [grammar/first-group-verbs-present.md](../../../grammar/first-group-verbs-present.md).

---

# Examples

- J'avance / nous avançons.
