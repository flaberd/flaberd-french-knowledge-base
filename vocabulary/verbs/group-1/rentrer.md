# Basic Information

## French

rentrer

## Part of Speech

Verb

## Ukrainian

повертатися додому

## English

to go home, to return home

# Forms *(if applicable)*

## Verb Group

Group 1 (regular -er)

## Past Participle

rentré *(regular -er → -é)*

---

# Notes

One of the "maison d'être" verbs (a compound of *entrer*, shown as "(r)entrer" in the "maison d'être" diagram) taking **être** as the passé composé auxiliary. First flagged in [lesson_00044](../../../lessons/lesson_00044.md)'s essay correction, where the teacher's bracketed note "(RENTRER!)" called for this verb instead of *aller* + chez moi; formally introduced here.
