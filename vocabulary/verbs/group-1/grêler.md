# Basic Information

## French

## Part of Speech

Verb

to hail

# Forms *(if applicable)*

## Verb Group

Group 1 (regular -er)

---

# Conjugation *(if applicable)*

Impersonal verb, used only in the third person singular with **il**: **il grêle** (it's hailing).

# Notes

Corrects the homework text "il fait pluie et il fait grêle" — French uses the dedicated verbs [pleuvoir](../group-3/pleuvoir.md) (il pleut) and grêler (il grêle), not "faire" + noun, for these weather phenomena.
