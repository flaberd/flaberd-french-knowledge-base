# Basic Information

## French

neiger

## Part of Speech

Verb

## Ukrainian

йти (про сніг)

## English

to snow

# Forms *(if applicable)*

## Verb Group

Group 1 (regular -er)

---

# Conjugation *(if applicable)*

Impersonal verb, used only in the third person singular with **il**: **il neige** (it's snowing/it snows). See [grammar/weather-expressions.md](../../../grammar/weather-expressions.md).

---

# Examples

- Il neige.
- Il neige parfois en décembre.
- Nous faisons du ski quand il neige.
