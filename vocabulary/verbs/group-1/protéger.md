# Forms *(if applicable)*

Group 1 (regular -er)

# Conjugation *(if applicable)*

Present tense: je protège, tu protèges, il/elle/on protège, nous protégeons, vous protégez, ils/elles protègent.

---

# Irregularities *(if applicable)*

-ger verb: the nous form inserts an extra "e" (protégeons) by the same pattern as manger/nager/voyager. See [grammar/first-group-verbs-present.md](../../../grammar/first-group-verbs-present.md). *(The é→è alternation in je/tu/il/ils forms is a further nuance not yet covered in the source lessons.)*
