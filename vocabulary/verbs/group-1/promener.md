# Basic Information

## French

promener

## Part of Speech

Verb

## Ukrainian

вигулювати

## English

to walk (someone/something, e.g. a dog)

# Forms *(if applicable)*

## Verb Group

Group 1 (regular -er, e→è stem-changing)

---

# Irregularities *(if applicable)*

Same e→è stem-change pattern as *acheter*/*mener*, also already seen in its reflexive form *se promener* (to go for a walk). See [grammar/er-verbs-stem-changes.md](../../../grammar/er-verbs-stem-changes.md) and [vocabulary/verbs/group-1/se-promener.md](se-promener.md).
