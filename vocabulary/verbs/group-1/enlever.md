# Basic Information

## French

enlever

## Part of Speech

Verb

## Ukrainian

знімати / прибирати

## English

to remove / to take off

# Forms *(if applicable)*

## Verb Group

Group 1 (regular -er)

# Conjugation *(if applicable)*

Present tense: j'enlève, tu enlèves, il/elle/on enlève, nous enlevons, vous enlevez, ils/elles enlèvent.

---

# Irregularities *(if applicable)*

Stem-changing verb, same pattern as *acheter*/*mener* (e→è). See [grammar/er-verbs-stem-changes.md](../../../grammar/er-verbs-stem-changes.md).
