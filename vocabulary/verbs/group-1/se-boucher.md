# Basic Information

## French

se boucher

## Part of Speech

Verb

## Ukrainian

затикати (собі)

## English

to block oneself, to plug/cover (something of one's own)

# Forms *(if applicable)*

## Verb Group

Group 1 (regular -er, reflexive)

# Examples

- "Alors tu te bouches plus les oreilles" *(from Stromae, "Alors on Danse")*
