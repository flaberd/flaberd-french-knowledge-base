Verb

# Forms *(if applicable)*

## Verb Group

Group 1 (regular -er)

---

# Conjugation *(if applicable)*

Present tense: je mange, tu manges, il/elle/on mange, **nous mangeons**, vous mangez, ils/elles mangent.

---

# Irregularities *(if applicable)*

-ger verb: the nous form inserts an extra "e" (mangeons, not mangons) to keep the "g" soft. This is the source's model example for the whole -ger pattern. See [grammar/first-group-verbs-present.md](../../../grammar/first-group-verbs-present.md).

---

# Examples

- Le chat mange sur le balcon.
- Nous ___ souvent. (manger)
- Nous ___ à midi. (manger)
