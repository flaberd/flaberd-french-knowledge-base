# Basic Information

passer

## Part of Speech

Verb

проходити, проводити (час)

## English

to pass, to go by, to spend (time)

# Examples

- En général, je passe la soirée avec Mike. → Hier, j'**ai passé** la soirée avec Mike. *(passé composé drill)*

---

# Notes

One of the "maison d'être" verbs — but note that *passer* is one of the few être-list verbs that can also take **avoir** when used with a direct object (e.g. "passer la soirée", "passer un examen"); only the être/no-object sense (to go by, to pass through) is covered by the "maison d'être" mnemonic. This nuance is not spelled out in the source, which lists *passer* plainly among the être verbs — flagged here for accuracy rather than silently applying être in all contexts.
