# Conjugation *(if applicable)*

Present tense: j'amène, tu amènes, il/elle/on amène, nous amenons, vous amenez, ils/elles amènent.

# Irregularities *(if applicable)*

Stem-changing verb, same pattern as *acheter*/*mener* (e→è). See [grammar/er-verbs-stem-changes.md](../../../grammar/er-verbs-stem-changes.md).
