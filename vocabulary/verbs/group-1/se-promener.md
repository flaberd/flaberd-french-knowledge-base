# Basic Information

## French

se promener

## Part of Speech

Verb

## Ukrainian

гуляти

## English

to go for a walk

# Forms *(if applicable)*

## Verb Group

Group 1 (regular -er, reflexive)

---

# Conjugation *(if applicable)*

Present tense: je me promène, tu te promènes, il/elle/on se promène, nous nous promenons, vous vous promenez, ils/elles se promènent.

---

# Irregularities *(if applicable)*

Stem-changing like *acheter*/*mener* (e→è), applied to the reflexive verb. See [grammar/er-verbs-stem-changes.md](../../../grammar/er-verbs-stem-changes.md).

---

# Examples

- Les parents de Julia ___ (se promener) le soir.
- Quand il fait beau, on se promène.
