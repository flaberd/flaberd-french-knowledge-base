Verb

# Forms *(if applicable)*

## Verb Group

Group 1 (-er, with a y → i spelling change before a silent e)

## Past Participle

envoyé *(regular -er → -é; the y→i change affects the present tense, not the participle)*

# Examples

- Tu (envoyer) ___ un message à tes amis. *(passé composé drill: "as envoyé")*

---

# Notes

Present-tense conjugation not yet taught in full, but note the spelling pattern: -yer verbs change y→i before a silent -e (j'envoie, tu envoies, il envoie, ils envoient) but keep y before -ons/-ez (nous envoyons, vous envoyez) — the same stem-change family as other -er irregularities. See [grammar/er-verbs-stem-changes.md](../../../grammar/er-verbs-stem-changes.md).
