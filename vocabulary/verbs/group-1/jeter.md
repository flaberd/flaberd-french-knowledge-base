# Basic Information

## French

jeter

## Part of Speech

Verb

## Ukrainian

кидати / викидати

## English

to throw (away)

# Forms *(if applicable)*

## Verb Group

Group 1 (regular -er)

---

# Conjugation *(if applicable)*

Present tense: je jette, tu jettes, il/elle/on jette, nous jetons, vous jetez, ils/elles jettent.

---

# Irregularities *(if applicable)*

Stem-changing verb: doubled consonant (tt) in all forms except nous/vous. See [grammar/er-verbs-stem-changes.md](../../../grammar/er-verbs-stem-changes.md).

---

# Examples

- Je jette la balle. Tu jettes le papier? Il jette toujours ses vêtements partout. Nous jetons le livre. Vous jetez la boîte? Elles jettent la pomme?
