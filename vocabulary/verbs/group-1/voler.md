# Basic Information

## French

voler

## Part of Speech

Verb

## Ukrainian

літати; красти

## English

to fly; to steal

# Forms *(if applicable)*

## Verb Group

Group 1 (regular -er)

## Past Participle

volé *(regular -er → -é)*

---

# Examples

- ...où quelqu'un (voler) mon sac. *(passé composé dialogue drill: "a volé" — the "to steal" sense here)*

---

# Notes

Two unrelated senses share this one spelling: **voler** = "to fly" (birds, planes) and, separately, "to steal" (as in this lesson's example). Both take **avoir** in the passé composé (regular -er verb, not one of the "maison d'être" set).
