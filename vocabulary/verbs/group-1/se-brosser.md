# Basic Information

## French

se brosser

## Part of Speech

Verb

## Ukrainian

чистити (щіткою)

## English

to brush (oneself / one's own)

# Forms *(if applicable)*

## Verb Group

Group 1 (regular -er, reflexive)

---

# Conjugation *(if applicable)*

Present tense: je me brosse, tu te brosses, il/elle/on se brosse, nous nous brossons, vous vous brossez, ils/elles se brossent.

---

# Fixed Expressions *(if applicable)*

- se brosser les dents — to brush one's teeth

---

# Examples

- Je me brosse les dents deux fois par jour.
