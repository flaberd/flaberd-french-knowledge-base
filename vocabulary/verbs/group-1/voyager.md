# Basic Information

## French

voyager

## Part of Speech

Verb

## Ukrainian

подорожувати

## English

to travel

# Forms *(if applicable)*

## Verb Group

Group 1 (regular -er)

---

# Conjugation *(if applicable)*

Present tense: je voyage, tu voyages, il/elle/on voyage, **nous voyageons**, vous voyagez, ils/elles voyagent.

---

# Irregularities *(if applicable)*

-ger verb: the nous form inserts an extra "e" (voyageons, not voyagons) to keep the "g" soft. See [grammar/first-group-verbs-present.md](../../../grammar/first-group-verbs-present.md).

---

# Examples

- voyager → nous voyageons.
- Vous ___ avec votre famille? (voyager)
- Elles ___ beaucoup. (voyager)
