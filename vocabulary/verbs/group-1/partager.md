# Conjugation *(if applicable)*

Present tense: je partage, tu partages, il/elle/on partage, **nous partageons**, vous partagez, ils/elles partagent.

---

# Irregularities *(if applicable)*

-ger verb: the nous form inserts an extra "e" (partageons, not partagons). See [grammar/first-group-verbs-present.md](../../../grammar/first-group-verbs-present.md).
