# Basic Information

## French

s'occuper (de)

## Part of Speech

Verb

## Ukrainian

піклуватися (про), займатися (чимось)

## English

to take care (of), to look after

# Forms *(if applicable)*

## Verb Group

Group 1 (regular -er, reflexive)

---

# Conjugation *(if applicable)*

Present tense: je m'occupe, tu t'occupes, il/elle/on s'occupe, nous nous occupons, vous vous occupez, ils/elles s'occupent.

---

# Required Prepositions *(if applicable)*

- s'occuper de + noun — to take care of / to look after something or someone

---

# Examples

- On s'occupe des plantes pendant votre absence.
