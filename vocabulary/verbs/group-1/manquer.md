# Basic Information

## French

manquer

## Part of Speech

Verb

## Ukrainian

бракувати, не вистачати

## English

to lack

# Forms *(if applicable)*

## Verb Group

Group 1 (regular -er)

---

# Conjugation *(if applicable)*

Present tense: je manque, tu manques, il/elle/on manque, nous manquons, vous manquez, ils/elles manquent.

---

# Required Prepositions *(if applicable)*

**manquer de** + noun (abstract/generic, no article): manquer **de** patience.

---

# Examples

- Elle **manque de** patience.

---

# Notes

One of the verbs taking "de" alone (no article) before an abstract/generic noun — see [grammar/partitive-articles.md](../../../grammar/partitive-articles.md#54-with-certain-verbs--abstractgeneric-noun).
