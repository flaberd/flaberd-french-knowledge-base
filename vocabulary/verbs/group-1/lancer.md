# Basic Information

## French

lancer

## Part of Speech

Verb

## Ukrainian

кидати / запускати

## English

to throw / to launch

# Forms *(if applicable)*

## Verb Group

Group 1 (regular -er)

---

# Conjugation *(if applicable)*

Present tense: je lance, tu lances, il/elle/on lance, nous lançons, vous lancez, ils/elles lancent.

---

# Irregularities *(if applicable)*

-cer verb: the nous form takes a cedilla (lançons). See [grammar/first-group-verbs-present.md](../../../grammar/first-group-verbs-present.md).

---

# Examples

- verbes en «-cer»: Commencer, lancer, avancer.
