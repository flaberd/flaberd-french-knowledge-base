# Basic Information

## French

aucun (aucune)

## Part of Speech

Determiner

## Ukrainian

жоден / жодна

## English

no, not any, none

# Forms *(if applicable)*

## Masculine

aucun

## Feminine

aucune

---

# Examples

- Il n'a aucun ami. Elle n'a aucune idée.

---

# Notes

Negative determiner, used with "ne" (ne...aucun/aucune) — agrees in gender with the noun it modifies, like an adjective. See [grammar/negation.md](../../grammar/negation.md).
