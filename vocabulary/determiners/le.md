# Basic Information

## French

le

## Part of Speech

Determiner

## Ukrainian

означений артикль (той самий / конкретний)

## English

the (definite article)

# Forms *(if applicable)*

## Masculine

le (l' before a vowel or mute h)

## Feminine

la (l' before a vowel or mute h)

## Plural

les

---

# Pronunciation *(if applicable)*

- Elision before a vowel or mute "h": le ami → l'ami ; le homme → l'homme.

---

# Examples

- la tour Eiffel, le Pont-Neuf, les bateaux-mouches, l'homme, la femme de Paul, les rues de Paris.
- Generality: la musique, le sport, le cinéma.

---

# Notes

Used for a unique/specific thing, or for a generality (a category taken as a whole). Contrast with the indefinite article — see [vocabulary/determiners/un.md](un.md).
