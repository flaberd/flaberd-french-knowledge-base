# Basic Information

## French

un

## Part of Speech

Determiner

## Ukrainian

неозначений артикль (один / якийсь)

## English

a / an (indefinite article)

# Forms *(if applicable)*

## Masculine

un

## Feminine

une

## Plural

des

---

# Pronunciation *(if applicable)*

- "un" + vowel-initial word → liaison in [n]: *un ami*, *un homme*.
- "des" + vowel-initial word → liaison in [z]: *des amis*, *des hommes*.

---

# Examples

- un homme, une femme, un arbre, une route, des arbres, des fleurs, des oiseaux.

---

# Notes

Used for a non-unique thing/person (a member of a category) or a quantity of one. Contrast with the definite article — see [vocabulary/determiners/le.md](le.md).
