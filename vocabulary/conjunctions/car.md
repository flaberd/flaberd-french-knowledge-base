# Basic Information

## French

car

## Part of Speech

Conjunction

## Ukrainian

бо, тому що (формальне)

## English

because (formal)

---

# Examples

- "Car les problèmes ne viennent pas seuls" *(from Stromae, "Alors on Danse")*

---

# Notes

Glossed in the source as the formal register of "because", contrasted with "parce que" (more common/neutral).
