# Basic Information

## French

bonsoir

## Part of Speech

Interjection

## Ukrainian

Добрий вечір

## English

good evening

---

# Fixed Expressions *(if applicable)*

Used as a greeting in the evening (contrast with [bonjour](bonjour.md), used until evening).
