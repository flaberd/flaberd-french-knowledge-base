# Basic Information

## French

comment ça va ?

## Part of Speech

Interjection

## Ukrainian

Як справи?

## English

how's it going? / how are you?

---

# Fixed Expressions *(if applicable)*

Also used in the short form **ça va ?** (or as a statement, **ça va**, meaning "it's going [fine]").

---

# Examples

- Comment ça va ? / Ça va — Як справи?

---

# Notes

The source PDF has a typo in the Ukrainian translation ("Як спрва?"); corrected to "Як справи?" here. This is the neutral/general form of asking how someone is doing — compare the informal [comment-vas-tu](comment-vas-tu.md) and formal [comment-allez-vous](comment-allez-vous.md).
