# Basic Information

## French

comment vas-tu ?

## Part of Speech

Interjection

## Ukrainian

Як ти?

## English

how are you? (informal)

---

# Notes

Informal register (uses **tu**), addressed to someone the speaker is familiar with. Compare the neutral [comment-ça-va](comment-ça-va.md) and formal [comment-allez-vous](comment-allez-vous.md).
