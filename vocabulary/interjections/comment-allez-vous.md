# Basic Information

## French

comment allez-vous ?

## Part of Speech

Interjection

## Ukrainian

Як Ви?

## English

how are you? (formal)

---

# Notes

Formal/polite register (uses **vous**), addressed to someone the speaker doesn't know well or wants to show respect to. Compare the neutral [comment-ça-va](comment-ça-va.md) and informal [comment-vas-tu](comment-vas-tu.md).
