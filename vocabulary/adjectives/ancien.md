# Basic Information

## French

ancien

## Part of Speech

Adjective

## Ukrainian

старий, давній, колишній

## English

old, former, ancient

# Forms *(if applicable)*

## Masculine

ancien

## Feminine

ancienne

---

# Examples

- une voiture ancienne.

---

# Notes

Meaning shifts with position: before the noun, *ancien* means "former" (e.g. *un ancien professeur* = a former teacher); after the noun, it means "ancient / old" (e.g. *une voiture ancienne* = an old/vintage car).
