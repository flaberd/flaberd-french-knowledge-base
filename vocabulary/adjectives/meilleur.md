# Examples

- Le café indien est **meilleur**. *(comparative of "bon")*
- Dites : Il est **meilleur**... Ne dites pas : Il est plus bon...

---

# Irregularities *(if applicable)*

Irregular comparative form of [bon](bon.md) (good) — used instead of ~~plus bon~~. Agrees in gender/number like a regular adjective (contrast with [mieux](../adverbs/mieux.md), the invariable comparative of the adverb *bien*). See [grammar/comparatif.md](../../grammar/comparatif.md#irregular-comparatives).
