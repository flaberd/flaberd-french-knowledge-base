# Notes

*Orange* is also a noun naming the fruit itself, but here it is used only as an invariable color adjective. Unlike *rouge*/*jaune* (which merely share one form for masculine/feminine because they end in -e), *orange* is a **true** invariable: it does not change in the plural either. See [grammar/color-agreement.md](../../grammar/color-agreement.md).
