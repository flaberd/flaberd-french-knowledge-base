rouge

## Part of Speech

Adjective

## Ukrainian

червоний

red

# Forms *(if applicable)*

## Masculine

rouge

## Feminine

rouge *(invariable)*

---

# Examples

- Je choisis le rouge. *(color-choice exercise)*
- Une écharpe rouge *(rouge ne change pas au féminin, ex. jaune, jeune)*
- Un chapeau __________. (rouge) *(color-agreement exercise: un chapeau rouge)*

---

# Notes

The feminine form is identical to the masculine simply because *rouge* already ends in unstressed -e (the regular pattern), not because it is a true invariable color like *orange*/*marron*. See [grammar/color-agreement.md](../../grammar/color-agreement.md).
