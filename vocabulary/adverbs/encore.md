# Basic Information

## French

encore

## Part of Speech

Adverb

## Ukrainian

ще, знову

## English

again, still

---

# Examples

- "Quand y en a plus et ben y en a encore" *(from Stromae, "Alors on Danse")*
- "Et là tu cries encore plus fort"

---

# Notes

Not to be confused with the frequency adverbs already documented in [grammar/adverbs-of-frequency.md](../../grammar/adverbs-of-frequency.md) (toujours, souvent, parfois...) — *encore* here means "still/again", not a frequency.
