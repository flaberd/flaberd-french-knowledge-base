# Basic Information

## French

mieux

## Part of Speech

Adverb

## Ukrainian

краще

## English

better

---

# Examples

- Fred danse **mieux**. *(comparative of "bien")*
- Il parle **mieux** français maintenant.

---

# Irregularities *(if applicable)*

Irregular comparative form of [bien](bien.md) (well) — used instead of ~~plus bien~~. Invariable, like all adverbs (contrast with [meilleur](../adjectives/meilleur.md), the comparative of the adjective *bon*, which agrees in gender/number). See [grammar/comparatif.md](../../grammar/comparatif.md#irregular-comparatives).
