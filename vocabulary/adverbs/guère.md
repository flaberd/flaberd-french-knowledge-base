# Basic Information

## French

guère

## Part of Speech

Adverb

## Ukrainian

майже не, ледве

## English

hardly, barely, scarcely

---

# Examples

- Vous ne mangez **guère** de viande rouge. *(ne...guère — a negation pattern meaning "hardly/barely")*

---

# Notes

Only used in the negative construction **ne...guère**; see [grammar/negation.md](../../grammar/negation.md#other-negation-patterns).
