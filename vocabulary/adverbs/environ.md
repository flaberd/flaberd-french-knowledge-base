# Basic Information

## French

environ

## Part of Speech

Adverb

## Ukrainian

приблизно

## English

around, about (approximate time/quantity)

---

# Examples

- à 7h, environ, à 8h30, vers 18h *(from the homework instructions, listing time expressions to use)*

---

# Notes

Used for approximation, similar in meaning to **vers** when used with time (vers 18h = "around 6pm"), but *environ* is more general (also used with quantities: environ dix minutes).
