# Basic Information

## French

plus

## Part of Speech

Adverb

## Ukrainian

більше не (у запереченні) / більше (порівняння)

## English

no longer, no more (in "ne...plus") — also "more" in comparisons (unrelated sense)

---

# Examples

- Je ne fume plus. Tu ne vas plus dire la vérité.

---

# Notes

Taught here specifically as the second half of the negation pattern **ne...plus** ("no longer/no more"), not in its separate comparative sense ("plus grand" = bigger). See [grammar/negation.md](../../grammar/negation.md).
