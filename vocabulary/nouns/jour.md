Noun

---

# Notes

**Jour vs. journée:** *jour* answers "quand ?" (which date/day) while [journée](journée.md) answers "combien de temps ?" (how much time/duration). Compare: "un beau jour" (a fine day, as a date) vs. "une bonne journée" (a good day, as a lived duration). A homework essay in [lesson_00026](../../lessons/lesson_00026.md) mixed the two ("mon jour ordinaire", "mon ordinaire la journée"), prompting this explicit nuance note from the teacher.
