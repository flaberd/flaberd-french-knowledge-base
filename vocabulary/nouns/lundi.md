# Basic Information

## French

le lundi

## Part of Speech

Noun

## Ukrainian

понеділок

## English

Monday

# Forms *(if applicable)*

## Masculine

lundi *(all days of the week are masculine)*

---

# Examples

- Le lundi, je me lève à sept heures et demie.
- Je travaille le lundi (every Monday) vs. Je travaille ce lundi (this Monday).
