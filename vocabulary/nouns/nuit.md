# Basic Information

## French

une nuit

## Part of Speech

Noun

## Ukrainian

ніч

## English

night

# Examples

- ___ nuit, il y a une éclipse de lune. *(fill-in exercise, left blank in source: "Cette nuit")*
- ___ nuit, je dors huit heures, en général. *(fill-in exercise, left blank in source: "La nuit")*

---

# Notes

Unlike "matin" and "soir" (masculine), "nuit" is feminine — takes "cette"/"la", not "ce"/"le".
