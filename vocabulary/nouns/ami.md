# Basic Information

## French

un ami

## Part of Speech

Noun

## Ukrainian

друг / подруга

## English

friend

# Forms *(if applicable)*

## Feminine

une amie

## Plural

des amis (m) / des amies (f)

---

# Pronunciation *(if applicable)*

- [ami]
- Liaison after "un": "un ami" → [œ̃.n‿ami].

---

# Examples

- Ils cherchent un ami amusant. — They are looking for a fun friend.
- ma amie → **mon amie** *(elision: "ma" becomes "mon" before a vowel-initial feminine noun)*
- Elle retrouve ___ amie. *(fill-in exercise, left blank in source: "son amie")*

---

# Notes

The feminine form "amie" follows the regular +e pattern, unlike homme/femme or monsieur/dame.
