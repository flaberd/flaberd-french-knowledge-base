# Basic Information

## French

l'Iran

## Part of Speech

Noun

## Ukrainian

Іран

## English

Iran

---

# Examples

- En + pays [pays commençant par une voyelle] : en Iran, en Irak. *(literal source example)*

---

# Notes

- Masculine country name, but begins with a vowel: takes **en** (not "au") for elision — l'Iran, en Iran.
