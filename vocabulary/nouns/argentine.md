# Basic Information

## French

l'Argentine

## Part of Speech

Noun

## Ukrainian

Аргентина

## English

Argentina

---

# Examples

- J'aime la Colombie / la Bolivie / l'Argentine. J'habite en Colombie / en Bolivie / en Argentine. *(literal source example, slide 4)*

---

# Notes

- Feminine country name, takes "l'" (elision) and the preposition "en" ("in/to Argentina").
