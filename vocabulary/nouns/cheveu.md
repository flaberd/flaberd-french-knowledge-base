# Basic Information

## French

un cheveu

## Part of Speech

Noun

## Ukrainian

волосина (одна волосинка); cheveux (мн.) = волосся

## English

a hair (single strand); cheveux (pl.) = hair

# Forms *(if applicable)*

## Plural

des cheveux

---

# Notes

The lesson only uses the plural "cheveux" (hair, collectively); the singular "un cheveu" (a single strand) is the canonical dictionary form recorded here but was not itself used in the source.
