# Basic Information

## French

la pâte

## Part of Speech

Noun

## Ukrainian

тісто / (les pâtes) макарони, паста

## English

dough, pastry / (les pâtes, usually plural) pasta

# Forms *(if applicable)*

## Feminine

la pâte

## Plural

les pâtes *(the plural form is the usual word for "pasta" as a food, distinct from the dough/pastry sense of the singular)*

---

# Examples

- il faut **de la pâte** *(pizza recipe: la pâte = dough)*
- les pâtes *(nationalités exercise, Les Italiens: Ils aiment les pâtes. Ils mangent des pâtes. — les pâtes = pasta)*

---

# Notes

Two related senses sharing one entry per the "no separate files for regular plural forms" rule: singular **la pâte** = dough/pastry (as in pizza dough), plural **les pâtes** = pasta (the dish).
