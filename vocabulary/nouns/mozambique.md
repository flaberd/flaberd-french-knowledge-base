# Basic Information

## French

le Mozambique

## Part of Speech

Noun

## Ukrainian

Мозамбік

## English

Mozambique

---

# Examples

- J'aime le Mozambique. J'habite au Mozambique. *(constructed, following the lesson's own pattern — not itself written out as a full sentence in the source)*

---

# Notes

- Masculine country name, takes "le" and the preposition "au" ("in/to Mozambique").
- Exception to the rule that country names ending in -e are usually feminine: **le Mozambique** is masculine despite the -e ending.
