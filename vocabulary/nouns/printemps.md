# Basic Information

## French

le printemps

## Part of Speech

Noun

## Ukrainian

весна

## English

spring

# Forms *(if applicable)*

## Masculine

printemps *(masculine, like all seasons — but takes "au", not "en", unlike the other three)*

---

# Examples

- au printemps
- Au printemps, il pleut souvent.
- Le festival de Cannes a lieu au printemps, au mois de mai.
- Les feuilles repoussent au printemps.

---

# Notes

Unlike the other three seasons (which take "en"), *printemps* takes **au**: "au printemps" vs. "en été / en automne / en hiver". See [grammar/months-and-seasons.md](../../grammar/months-and-seasons.md).
