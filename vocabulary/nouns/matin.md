un matin

## Part of Speech

Noun

## Ukrainian

ранок

## English

morning

# Examples

- Ce matin, je suis malade, je reste à la maison. *(model given in source)*
- ___ matin, d'habitude, je vais au bureau. *(fill-in exercise, left blank in source: "Le matin")*

---

# Notes

Masculine time-of-day noun; part of the "le/la vs. ce/cette + moment de la journée" exercise contrasting habitual ("le matin" = in the morning, generally) vs. specific ("ce matin" = this morning) reference.
