# Notes

Exception to the -teur/-eur = masculine tendency: many nouns ending in **-eur** are actually feminine, e.g. *la chaleur*, *la fleur*, *la couleur*, *la peur*, *la valeur*. See [grammar/noun-gender-endings.md](../../grammar/noun-gender-endings.md).
