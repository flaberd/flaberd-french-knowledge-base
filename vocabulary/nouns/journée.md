# Notes

**Journée vs. jour:** *journée* answers "combien de temps ?" (how much time/duration) while [jour](jour.md) answers "quand ?" (which date/day). A homework essay in [lesson_00026](../../lessons/lesson_00026.md) mixed the two ("mon jour ordinaire", "mon ordinaire la journée" — the second also has the adjective in the wrong position; the natural phrasing is "ma journée ordinaire", adjective after the noun), prompting this explicit nuance note from the teacher.
