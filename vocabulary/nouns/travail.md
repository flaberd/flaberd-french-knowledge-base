# Notes

Irregular plural: -ail → -aux (like vitrail → vitraux, bail → baux), unlike regular -ail nouns such as détail → détails.

**Travail vs. travaille:** *travail* is the noun (work), *travailler* is the verb (to work) — "travaille" is only correct as a conjugated verb form (je/tu/il travaille), never as the noun. A homework essay in [lesson_00026](../../lessons/lesson_00026.md) mixed the two ("pour le travaille" instead of "pour le travail").
