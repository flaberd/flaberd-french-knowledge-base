# Basic Information

## French

le Zimbabwe

## Part of Speech

Noun

## Ukrainian

Зімбабве

## English

Zimbabwe

---

# Examples

- J'aime le Zimbabwe. J'habite au Zimbabwe. *(constructed, following the lesson's own pattern — not itself written out as a full sentence in the source)*

---

# Notes

- Masculine country name, takes "le" and the preposition "au" ("in/to Zimbabwe").
- Exception to the rule that country names ending in -e are usually feminine: **le Zimbabwe** is masculine despite the -e ending.
