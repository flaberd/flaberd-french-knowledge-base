## Part of Speech

## Ukrainian

## English

---

# Notes

Takes a plural **-s** on "cents" when it is a round multiple of a hundred with nothing following it; the **-s** is dropped as soon as another number follows (e.g. deux-cent-deux). See [grammar/numbers-80-and-above.md](../../grammar/numbers-80-and-above.md).
