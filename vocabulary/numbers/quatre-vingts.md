# Basic Information

## French

quatre-vingts

## Part of Speech

Number

## Ukrainian

вісімдесят

## English

eighty

---

# Notes

Literally "four twenties." Takes a plural **-s** when it is a round multiple of twenty with nothing following it; the **-s** is dropped as soon as another number follows (e.g. quatre-vingt-un). See [grammar/numbers-80-and-above.md](../../grammar/numbers-80-and-above.md).
