# Notes

The **-s** on "cent" (seen in *deux cents* = 200) is dropped as soon as another number follows. Spelled with hyphens throughout per the post-1990 rectified orthography shown in the source chart. See [grammar/numbers-80-and-above.md](../../grammar/numbers-80-and-above.md).
